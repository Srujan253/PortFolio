# Brand & Design System

## Core Theme
**Aesthetic:** Modern, Cyberpunk, Glassmorphic, High-Tech.
The portfolio utilizes a dark, immersive theme (`bg-gray-950`) heavily accented with glowing elements, floating orbs, and glassmorphism panels to create a "Command Center" or "Digital Space" feel.

## Colors
- **Background:** Deep dark slate (`#0f172a` to `#030712`).
- **Primary Accents:** Cyan (`#22d3ee`) and Electric Blue (`#3b82f6`).
- **Secondary Accents:** Emerald Green (Success), Purple (Tech), Yellow (Gaming/Features).
- **Text:** White (`#ffffff`) for headings, Light Gray (`#9ca3af`) for descriptions.

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter / sans-serif (clean, highly readable, standard for tech interfaces).
- **Headings:** Bold/Black weights (`font-black`) often paired with `text-transparent bg-clip-text bg-gradient-to-r` for glowing text effects.
- **Body:** Standard weights with high line-height (`leading-relaxed`) for readability.

## UI Elements
- **Glassmorphism (`.glass`):** Used extensively for cards and panels (`bg-white/10 backdrop-blur-md border border-white/20`).
- **Icons:** `react-icons` (Fa, Si) used heavily. Icons often sit inside custom circular/square containers with a slight `bg-opacity` to match the brand color of the respective technology.
- **Animations:** Powered by `framer-motion`. Includes staggered fades (`opacity`), slide-ups (`y: 50`), spring-based scale hover effects, and 3D tilts (for skills/cards).

## The "Srujan H M" Logo
- **Favicon/Logo Idea:** A neon cyan and blue "HM" monogram on a pure black background. Sleek, symmetrical, vector-style.
